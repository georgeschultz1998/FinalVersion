About Us

Our real estate company has been helping people find affordable housing for several years. We offer among the lowest rent in North Carolina and are family-owned. Our founder and owner, Erick Schultz, takes great pride in providing comfortable and convenient housing options for individuals and families alike. Our apartments are located in the charming town of Canton, nestled in the beautiful mountains of Western North Carolina, just a short drive from the vibrant city of Asheville. We're dedicated to providing our residents with a comfortable and welcoming home, and we look forward to serving you.
